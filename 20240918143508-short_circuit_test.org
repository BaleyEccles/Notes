* Short Circuit Test
Short the secondary side of a [[id:89a05d8d-08a9-4ac8-81bc-78239de5bc5c][Transformer]] and measure
 - \[P_{2c}\]
 - \[V_{1}\]
 - \[I_{1}\]
Which will be used to find
 - \[R_{eq}=\frac{P_{sc}}{I_1^2}\]
 - \[Z_{eq}=\frac{V_1}{I_1}\]
 - \[X_{eq}=\sqrt{Z_{eq}^2-R_{eq}^{2}\]
