* Newton-Raphson Method
A [[id:5d1b6d40-17f3-486c-9aae-bd5516083d30][Fixed Point Method]] that uses the operation \[x_{n+1}=x_n-\frac{f(x_n)}{f'(x_n)}\].
** Convergence
Quadratic convergence
