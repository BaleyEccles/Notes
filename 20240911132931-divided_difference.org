* Divided Difference
\[f[x_i,x_{i+1},...x_{i+k}]=\frac{f[x_{i+1},x_{i+2},...x_{i+k}]-f[x_i,x_{i+1},...x_{i+k-1}]}{x_{i+k}-x_{i}}\]
