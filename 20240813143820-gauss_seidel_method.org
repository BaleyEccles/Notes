* Gauss-Seidel Method
In matrix form
- \[x^{(k+1)}=L^{-1}(b-Ux^{(k)}\]
In element form
- \[x_i^{(k+1)}=\frac{1}{a_{ii}}(b_i-\sum_{j=1,j\ne i}^{n}a_{ij}x_j)\]
This method will converge if:
- \[A\] is diagonally dominant.
- This method may also converge if this isnt true.
