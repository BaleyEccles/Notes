* White Noise
 - $X(t)$ is a white noise signal if:
   - \[S_{XX}(\omega)=\frac{\eta}{2}\]
   - \[\Rightarrow R_{XX}(\tau)=\frac{\eta}{2}\delta(\tau)\]
