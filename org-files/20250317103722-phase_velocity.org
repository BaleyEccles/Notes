* Phase Velocity
 - \[u_p = frac{dz}{dt} = \frac{\omega}{k} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{\mu\epsilon}}\]
 - In free space $c = u_p = 2.9979\times 10^8 \approx 3\times 10^8$
