* Hilbert Transform
Defined as:
 - \[\hat{x}(t) = x(t)*\frac{1}{\pi t}\]
Introduces a $-\frac{\pi}{2}$ phase shift at positvie frequencies
And a $+\frac{\pi}{2}$ phase shift at negative frequencies
