* Bisection Method
We halve the interval with \[c=\frac{a+b}{2}\], now \[x^{*}\] will be in \[[a,c]\] or \[[c,b]\]. If \[f(c)=0\] then \[c=x^{*}\] and we have found the root.
** Convergence
This method converges linearly
