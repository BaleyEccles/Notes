* Signal To Noise Ratio (SNR)
 - The ratio from signal to noise
 - \[\frac{S}{N}\]
   - Signal power divide by noise power
 - Using the [[id:cfec5fb5-467e-4a16-a78c-32ebbdd4fb9a][Channel Noise]]
   - \[S_{NN}(f) = \frac{N_0}{2}\]
 - Signal
   - \[X_0(t)\]
     - with a power $P_R$
 - \[\frac{S}{N} = \frac{E[X_0^2(t)]}{E[n_0^2(t)} = \frac{P_R}{N_0W}\]
