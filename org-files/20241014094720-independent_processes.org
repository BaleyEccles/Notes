* Independent Processes
 - \[f_{XY}(x,y)=f_X(x)f_Y(y)\]
 - They are uncorrelated for all $\tau$ if:
   - \[C_{XY}(\tau)=R_{XY}(\tau)-\mu_X\mu_Y=0\]
 - Orthogonal if:
   - \[R_{XY}(\tau)=0\]
