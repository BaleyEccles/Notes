* Nyquist-Shannon Capacity Theorem
 - $c = w\log_{2}\left( 1 + \frac{P}{wN_0}\right)$
   - $c$: max bits/second
   - $wN_0$: Noise power
   - $P$: Signal power
 - $\frac{P}{wN_0}$: Signal to noise ratio
