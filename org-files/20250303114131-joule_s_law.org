* Joule’s law
 - \[P=\int_vE\cdot Jdv\]
   - Measured in Watts
- Using the [[id:470e50aa-5154-4107-9a2f-3a838f5b4fd6][Conductance]] equation ($J=\sigma E$)
  - \[P=\int_v\sigma|E|^2dv\]
