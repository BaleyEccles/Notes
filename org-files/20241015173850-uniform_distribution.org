* Uniform Distribution
 - \[f(x)=\begin{cases}
   \frac{1}{b-a}, & if\ a\leq x \leq b\\
   0, & else
   \end{cases}\]
   - \[\mu=\frac{b-a}{2}\]
   - \[\sigma^2=\frac{(b-a)^{2}}{12}\]
