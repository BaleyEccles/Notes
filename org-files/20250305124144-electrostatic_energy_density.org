* Electrostatic Energy Density
 - Given by the volume divided by the [[id:afaf1f4e-547f-4669-9bdb-74247c479c07][Electrostatic Potential Energy]]
   - \[w_e=\frac{W_e}{v}}\]
     - Where $v$ is the volume, no the voltage
