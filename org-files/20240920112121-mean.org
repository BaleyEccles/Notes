* Mean
 - The expected value
   - Doesn't have to exist as possible outcome
   - Expected value:
     - \[E[X]=\sum_{x}xP(X=x)\]
       - $x$ can be anything, just substitute into the $x$
       - eg. $E[f(x)]$
