* Doppler Effect
 - The frequency changes depending on how the receiver and transmitter are moving
 - \[f_c + f_d \]
   - \[f_d = \frac{v}{c}f_c\]
 - Increases bandwidth
   - \[B_{Doppler} = B_{static} + 2|f_{d,\max}|\]
