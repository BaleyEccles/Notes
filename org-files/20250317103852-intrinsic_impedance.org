* Intrinsic Impedance
 - \[\eta = \frac{\omega \mu}{k}= \sqrt{\frac{\epsilon}{\mu}}\]
 - Intrinsic impedance of free space
   - \[\eta_0 = \sqrt{\epsilon_0\mu_0} \approx 377\Omega\]

** In Lossy Media
 - \[\eta_c = \sqrt{\frac{\mu}{\epsilon_c}} = \sqrt{\frac{\mu}{\epsilon' - j \epsilon''}}\]
