** Notation
 - Denoted with a uppercase letter and time ($X(t)$)
 - Example [[id:8904baec-0390-4296-b7e6-9ef4ede346a5][PDF]]: $X(t)=a\cos(\omega_0t+\Theta)$
   - Here $\Theta$ (uppercase) is the random value that takes a sample $\theta$ (lowercase).
