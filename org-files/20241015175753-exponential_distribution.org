* Exponential Distribution
 - \[f(x)=\lambda e^{-\lambda x}\]
 - \[F(x)=1-e^{-\lambda x}\]
 - \[E[X]=\frac{1}{\lambda}\]
 - \[Var(X)=\frac{1}{\lambda^2}\]
