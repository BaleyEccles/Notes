* Unit Ramp
Denoted \[r (t)\].
Defined as \[\begin{math} r(t)= \begin{cases}
        t & t > 0 \\
        0 & t < 0 \end{cases}
\end{math}\]
Related to the [[id:df314ae5-1b35-49a3-be34-8d0aeb720ff3][Unit Step]] by \[r(t)=tu(t)=\int_{-infty}^{t}u(\lambda)d\lambda\]
