* Unit Step
\begin{math}
u(t)=
\begin{cases}
        1 & t > 0 \\
        0 & t < 0
\end{cases}
\end{math}.
Relation to [[id:31e21e4b-6463-4efb-a9bd-6fb1f20d5db8][Delta Function]]:
\[u(t)=\int_{-\infty}^{t}\delta (\lambda)d\lambda \Leftrightarrow \delta (t)=\frac{du(t)}{dt}\].
