* Unit Pulse
Denoted \[\Pi (t)\].
Defined as:
\[\Pi(t)= \begin{cases}
        1 & \lvert t\rvert < \frac{1}{2} \\
        0 & \lvert t\rvert > \frac{1}{2}
\end{cases}\]

or in terms of the [[id:df314ae5-1b35-49a3-be34-8d0aeb720ff3][Unit Step]] as \[\Pi (t)=u(t+\frac{1}{2})-u(t-\frac{1}{2})\].
