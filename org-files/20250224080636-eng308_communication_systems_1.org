* Overview
\[|\textrm{Infomation source}|\rightarrow |\textrm{Transmitter}| \rightarrow\ |\textrm{Channel}| \rightarrow |\textrm{Reciver}| \rightarrow \textrm{output}\]
** Transmitter (Tx)
 - map to wave form
   - Modulate
** Channel
 - This is where the problems occur
   - Interferance
   - Noise
   - Dispersion
** Reciver (Rx)
 - Reverse the mapping
   - Demodulation
 - Detect
